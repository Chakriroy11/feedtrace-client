#  FeedTrace Client
> **Empowering Shoppers with AI-Verified Product Reviews & Transparency**

FeedTrace is a modern e-commerce discovery platform designed to eliminate "fake review" culture. By utilizing AI-powered bill verification, FeedTrace ensures that every review you read comes from a legitimate owner of the product.

---

##  The Core Innovation: AI Verification
Most platforms allow anyone to leave a review. FeedTrace changes the game:
* **Proof of Purchase:** Users upload their invoice/bill when submitting a review.
* **AI Analysis:** The backend scans the document to verify the Order ID, Purchase Date, and Product Name.
* **Trust Score:** Every review is assigned a **Trust Score (%)**. High scores indicate the AI is confident in the purchase authenticity.

---

##  Key Features

### 1. Verified Review Ecosystem
* **Trust Badges:** Products with verified reviews receive a "Shield" badge to signal safety.
* **Contextual Feedback:** Includes "Usage Context" (e.g., *Why I bought this*, *Favorite feature*) for deeper insights.
* **Contribution Tracker:** A dedicated "My Reviews" dashboard to track verification status and AI scores.

### 2. Premium Discovery Experience
* **Dynamic Catalog:** Browse products across multiple categories with real-time brand filtering.
* **Smart Variants:** Intuitive UI for switching between product colors and storage sizes with instant price updates.
* **Responsive Gallery:** High-quality image exploration for every product.

### 3. Modern Auth & Profile
* **Unified Design:** Symmetrical, gradient-based Login and Signup experience.
* **Personalization:** Manage profile details and custom avatars.

---

##  Mobile-First Experience
Built for the shopper on the move, FeedTrace is 100% responsive:
* **App-Like Navigation:** Floating bottom navigation bar for quick access.
* **Fluid Layouts:** Product details and review forms adapt perfectly to small screens.
